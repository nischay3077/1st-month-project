Dale Willem Steyn, or the "Steyn Gun," was a force of nature. For a record 263 weeks, he sat atop the ICC Test rankings. With a combination of 150kmph pace and late-swing, he dismantled batting lineups across the globe, leaving behind a legacy of aggression and absolute technical mastery.
